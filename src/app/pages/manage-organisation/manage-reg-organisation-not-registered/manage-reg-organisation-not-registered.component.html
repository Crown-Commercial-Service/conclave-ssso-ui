Set up an administrator for your organisation

You need at least one administrator for your account.

More information on being an administrator

Being an administrator means you have full rights to all settings and services on this account. You can:

  • add, access and delete any data
  • add other administrators
  • set up 'users' - people who can use the service, but with limited access compared to you

We recommend consulting your colleagues and planning who should be administrators. Once you have chosen, be sure to share who is doing the role with the people who will need to know within your team.
