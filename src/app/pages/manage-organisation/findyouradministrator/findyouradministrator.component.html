How to find your administrator

Your administrator is someone who work for your organisation. We can't tell you exactly who your administrator is but here are a few ideas of who to ask.

Your administrator might be:

  • The administrator of your organisation's account
  • Responsible for managing users and creating groups
  • Able to edit your organisation's details
  • The sole admin user, although you can add more
