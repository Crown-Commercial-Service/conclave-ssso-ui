Register your organisation

Your organisation is not registered. You will need to register your organisation before you can create an individual account.

To continue, register with us to create a secure account for your organisation and access all our services in one place. You cannot create an individual account until your organisation is registered. If you choose not to be an administrator you can nominate a colleague.

When you register, you will be:

  • The administrator of your organisation's account
  • Responsible for managing users and creating groups
  • Able to edit your organisation's details
  • The sole admin user, although you can add more

Registering takes around 10 minutes.
