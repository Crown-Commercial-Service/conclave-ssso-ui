New User Instructions


As an administrator, you need an authenticator app on your phone or tablet.

The app generates a passcode that you will need to log in each time you sign in. This is a very secure way to log in and is important to protect your organisation.

  • Go to your device’s app store and search for one of the following free apps:

    • Microsoft Authenticator
    • Google Authenticator
    • Auth0 Guardian
    • Authy

  • Follow the instructions on screen to download the app.

  • When it has downloaded, open the app.
    Use the app to scan the QR code on the right hand side of this page.

  • Enter the passcode displayed on your device, in the box under the QR code on the right hand side of this page.

I cannot download the app
If you cannot download the app, you can have a passcode sent to you by text message. To do this, select the option ‘I’d rather use SMS’ on the right hand side of this page.

The authenticator app will not have access to your personal or government information.
