PPG User Administrator

When registering on the PPG an Organisation must designate one individual User as the Organisation’s Administrator.

The Administrator is responsible for managing their Organisation’s PPG Account, including appointing other Users as Administrators.

Administrators, as Controllers of their Organisation's data, are responsible for the management of their Organisation’s PPG Accounts. When a registered User/s leaves an Organisation, the Administrator must disable their account. The Administrator must also submit a request in writing for CCS to disable the User/s account.

Login and password are strictly personal to the User/s and are non-transferable. Each User/s shall ensure they do not divulge or disclose their password to third -7-parties. In the event that a User/s becomes aware a password may have been divulged, disclosed or discovered by any third party, they shall contact their Administrator who shall take immediate action to rectify the situation.

Users accessing the PPG

User/s must use their Organisation’s email address to access the PPG. A User may access the PPG using personal email accounts such as Hotmail, Gmail, Google Mail if that is the email that the Organisation utilises for its normal operations.
