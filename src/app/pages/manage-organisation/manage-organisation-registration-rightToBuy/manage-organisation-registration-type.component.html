Are you a supplier, buyer or both?

Select the organisation you work for

Your organisation is a supplier if you supply goods to CCS buyers
Your organisation is a buyer if you buy goods using CCS
Your organisation is both if you buy and sell goods using CCS
How we define both

An example of both may be:

  • You buy goods and services from CCS, and you also supply goods and services to other CCS buyers.

You are not both if:

  • You supply to a CCS buyer, but you buy from third parties to meet the agreement. In other words,

    periphery arrangements with third parties or subcontractors for example, are not relevant to this definition.
