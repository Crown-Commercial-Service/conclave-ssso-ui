How to find your administrator

It will be someone who works for your organisation. We can't tell you who your administrator is, but you should ask:

  • the person who gave you your work email account
  • someone in your IT or HR department
  • your direct line manager
  • someone in your procurement team
  • the person who manages your email service or website
