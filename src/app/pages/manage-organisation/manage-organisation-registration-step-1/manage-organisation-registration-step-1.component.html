Create the account for your organisation

We need to register your organisation to make sure your account is secure.
You cannot create an individual acccount until your organisation is registered


When you register, you will be:

  • The administrator of your organisation's account
  • Responsible for managing users and creating groups
  • Able to edit your organisation's details
  • The sole admin user, although you can add more

Registering takes around 10 minutes

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Before you start

If you do not wish to become the administrator of your organisation's
account you can nominate a colleague to register and become an administrator.
This is a permanent position, but can be passed on to others.
