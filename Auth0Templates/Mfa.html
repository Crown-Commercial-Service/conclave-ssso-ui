Additional security

To proceed as an administrator you will need an authentication app on your device. An authentication app will generate a passcode that you can use each time you sign in. This app will not have access to your personal or government information.

If it is not possible for you to use an authentication app then you can have a passcode sent to your mobile device in an SMS message. For this option select ‘I’d rather use SMS’.

  • Go to your device’s app store and search for an authenticator app such as one of the following free to download apps:

    • Auth0 Guardian
    • Google Authenticator
    • Microsoft Authenticator
    • Twilio Authy

  • Select and install an authentication app following the on screen instructions. Once installed open the app.

  • Using the app scan the QR code on this page and enter the passcode displayed on your device.
